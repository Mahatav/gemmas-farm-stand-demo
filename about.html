Our story

A stand at the end of the driveway

Gemma's Farm Stand is a small, family-run produce stand on Galiano Road in Kelowna. This page is the short version of how it came to be.

It started with too many tomatoes

Every grower knows the problem: some weeks the garden gives you more than any one family can eat. For years the extra went to neighbours, coworkers, and anyone who left their car window open.

The stand is the grown-up version of that habit. In 2026 we got our City of Kelowna business licence, put up a proper table at 2231 Galiano Rd, and started selling what the field produces each week.

There's no warehouse and no supplier list. If a late frost takes the apricots, there are no apricots that week. What you see on the table is what the season actually did.

How we work

Three rules we don't break

Picked, then sold

Nothing sits. Produce goes on the table the same week it comes off the plant, usually the same day.

Grown here

Everything on the stand is grown on this property or by growers we know personally in the Okanagan.

Priced fair

No middlemen means we can charge less than the grocery store for better produce, and still come out ahead.
